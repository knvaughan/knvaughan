Services

I provide ASL/English interpretation services for individuals in the Deaf and Hard of Hearing communities in the Charlotte, NC area. My services are available in-person or virtual via Zoom.

Duties

As a freelance interpreter, my responsibilities during all my assignments are as follows:

  • Arrive at the assignment on time and prepared.
  • Conduct and present myself in a professional and ethical manner with regard to the setting and the consumer.
  • Facilitate effective communication between all parties.
  • Satisfy the preferred mode of communication for consumers both receptively and expressively.
  • Adhere to the RID Code of Ethics with special consideration to Confidentiality.
  • Maintain North Carolina Interpreter and Transliterator License.

Cost

My regular rate is $50.00 per hour. This rate will be charged for work conducted from 8am until 5pm, Monday through Friday.

My enhanced rate is $75.00 per hour. This rate will be charged for work conducted from 5pm until 8am, Monday through Friday, weekends, and holidays, unless otherwise noted prior to the appointment time.

For all-day assignments, payment is expected for the entire duration of the day – including lunch breaks – unless prior arrangements have been made in writing.

Travel and Parking Fees can be negotiated. Unless otherwise stated, standard mileage reimbursement rate is $0.56 per mile following the IRS’ current rate. Any parking costs incurred will be included in the invoice.

All assignments have a two-hour minimum booking requirement. This applies to last minute cancellations and if the client does not show. Any appointments that run longer than two hours will be billed in 15 minute increments. All assignments cancelled with less than 24 hours’ notice are billed at the full contract rate.
